6 times Sushmita Sen proved that she is a woman of substance

Sushmita Sen (born 19 November 1975)[1][2] is an Indian actress, model and beauty queen. She won the Miss Universe 1994 pageant, after previously winning Femina Miss India at age 18. Since her pageant wins, she has worked as an actress.


Single mother to two adopted girls


  • When she was only 24 years old and single, Sen adopted a baby girl and made her the centre of her life.

  • Ten years later, she adopted another girl. Renee and Alisah are growing up to be confident and strong-minded girls, just like their beautiful mother.


  • About her decision to adopt, Sen has said in an earlier interview that while many considered it to be an act of charity, for her, it was self-conservation. The single mother believes her children were born from her heart and that is possibly the purest relationship there can be.
